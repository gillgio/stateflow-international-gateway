Three Gateways

Choose the pathway that matches your assessment reality.

StateFlow organises language assessment intelligence into three entry systems: global academic routes, Cambridge-aligned development pathways, and Taiwan institutional deployment.

□

Academic Global Gateway

IELTS Academic / TOEFL iBT

For international study, research pathways, academic communication, and premium diagnostic review.

●Diagnostic Purpose: Academic readiness and communication evidence beyond practice-test scores.

Cambridge Ecosystem Gateway

A2–C2 / CEFR / Young Learners / Adult Pathways

For long-term English development, age-appropriate progression, and school or programme-level diagnostic planning.

●Diagnostic Purpose: Proficiency mapping and learning progression for Cambridge-aligned pathway candidates.

Explore Cambridge Pathway Coming soon  → gateway_id: cambridge_ecosystem

Not an official Cambridge partnership or official assessment service.

Taiwan Institutional Gateway

TOEIC L/R / GSAT English / GEPT Intermediate

For schools, enterprises, local institutions, workplace English, and Taiwan-based diagnostic deployment.

●Diagnostic Purpose: Route-native evidence, report outputs, and repair direction aligned with local assessment needs.
